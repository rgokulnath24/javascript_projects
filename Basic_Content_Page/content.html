Actor

An actor is a performer who portrays characters in various productions, including theatre, film, television, and other media. They interpret scripts, research roles, and embody characters to entertain, inform, or instruct audiences. Actors use their voice, body language, and expressions to bring characters to life and connect with audiences emotionally.

Politician

A politician is someone who participates in the process of making decisions that affect a group of people, often within a government or political system. They are involved in the formulation and implementation of policies, representing the interests of their constituents and influencing public policy.

Engineer

An engineer applies scientific and mathematical principles to design, develop, and maintain systems, structures, and processes across various industries. They use their technical expertise to create products and solutions that solve problems and meet organizational objectives. Engineers often collaborate with other teams, conduct tests, and ensure their designs are safe, functional, and meet industry standards.

Director

A film director, also known as a filmmaker, is the creative leader responsible for bringing a screenplay to life. They oversee the artistic and dramatic aspects of a film, guiding the actors and crew to achieve their vision. This involves interpreting the script, selecting actors, managing production design, and directing the overall look and feel of the movie.
